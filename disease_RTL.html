The Impact of Lifestyle on Health

Lifestyle diseases are primarily caused by unhealthy habits such as poor diet, lack of exercise, and stress. Understanding these diseases can lead to better choices and improved health.

Obesity

Definition: A condition characterized by excessive body fat.

Causes: Poor diet, lack of exercise, and genetic factors.

Prevention: Regular exercise, a balanced diet, and maintaining a healthy lifestyle.

Heart Disease

Definition: A range of conditions affecting the heart, such as coronary artery disease.

Causes: High cholesterol, smoking, obesity, and lack of physical activity.

Prevention: Regular exercise, a healthy diet, and avoiding tobacco use.

Diabetes

Definition: A condition where the body cannot properly process blood sugar.

Causes: Poor diet, obesity, and lack of physical activity.

Prevention: Maintaining a healthy diet and regular exercise.
