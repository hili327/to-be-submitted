What Are Physical Diseases?

Physical diseases encompass a wide range of conditions that affect the body's functions and structures. These disorders can impact an individual's daily life, overall health, and well-being.

Common physical health disorders include diabetes, hypertension, heart diseases, and arthritis. Understanding these conditions is essential for promoting health and providing support to those in need.
